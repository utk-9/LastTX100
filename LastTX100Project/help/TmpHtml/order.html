Story:

The pirate that once feared by all has no respect now. The pirate whole command was more powerful than a Lion's roar goes unheard now. The pirate whose area under control extended from the far waters of the West to the those in the deep East now merely survives on a tiny collection of small islands.

Why, you ask?

His girl, future queen of his pirate empire, was kidnapped. Despite his multiple attempts, he hasn't been able to succeed in his quest to find her. However, he is ready to give it one more try and play on his life to search for his love.
